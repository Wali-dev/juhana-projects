Common Mental Health Problems You Should Know About

Before looking for the solutions to an illness, it is important to understand the disease type itself. Below are some common issues among youth mental health, explained simply.

Depression

Depression is a mood disorder caused by imbalances of brain chemicals like serotonin and dopamine, affecting how you feel, think, and handle daily activities.

Signs
  • Constant sadness or emptiness
  • Insomnia or hypersomnia
  • Loss of energy

Anxiety

Anxiety disorders involve excessive fear or worry, often triggered by everyday situations. It can interfere with work, school, and relationships.

Signs
  • Excessive worrying
  • Restlessness
  • Increased heart rate
