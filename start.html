Common Mental Health Problems You should Know About

Before looking for the solutions of an illness, it is required to know abo ut the disease type itelf.So below are some of the most common issues prevailing among youth' mental health with a short overview and easy for you to understand.

Depression

Depression is a mood disorder caused by imbalances of brain chemicals like serotonin and dopamine, affecting how you feel, think, and handle daily activities.

Signs
  • Constant sadness or emptiness
  • Insomnia or Hypersomnia
  • Loss of energy

Schizophrenia

Schizophrenia is linked to imbalances in brain chemicals like dopamine and glutamate, causing hallucinations, delusions, and disorganized thinking.

Signs
  • Hearing voices
  • Confused speech
  • Withdrawal from others

Eating Disorders

Eating disorders like anorexia and bulimia are tied to emotional stress, body image issues, and dysfunction in the reward system of the brain.

Signs
  • Extreme dieting or binge eating
  • Fear of gaining weight
  • Preoccupation with appearance

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder

PTSD develops after very stressful events, as the brain struggles to process trauma. The hippocampus (memory center) and amygdala (fear center) are heavily involved.

Signs

  • Flashbacks
  • Nightmares
  • Hypervigilance

Attention Deficit Hyper Disorder

ADHD involves underactivity in parts of the brain that control focus and impulse, especially the prefrontal cortex.

Signs

  • Difficulty paying attention
  • Impulsiveness
  • Hyperactivity

Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder involves unusual changes in mood, energy, and concentration due to fluctuations in neurotransmitter activity like dopamine and norepinephrine.

Signs

  • Extreme happiness followed by deep sadness
  • Racing thoughts
  • Risky behavior
